Rest of Season Analysis¶

This season has seen a pretty wide spread of records, with a couple of teams virtually guaranteed to either make the playoffs or contend for avoiding placement of The Austin on their mantle for a year. This report uses our cumulative performance and current standings to predict each team's chances of making the championship, winning the playoffs, or getting last place. This is accomplished through simulating millions of matchups based on the remaining season schedule.

Discussion¶

Anyone's game¶

There are six weeks left in the regular season, and nearly every team has a shot at making the championships (sorry, Snoz Berries). These last six weeks are still important for your fantasy team. Shore up your weak spots with some clever waiver picks.

A Sure Bet¶

Congratulations to the Screw City Scramblers, No Shit Sherlock, and Touchdown Jesus Arsonist. These teams have nearly cemented their spots in the playoffs, each with a 95% chance of making it. Things are looking particularly good for the Screw City Scramblers, who are currently projected as most likely to win the championship (36% of outcomes).

A Strong Edge¶

The King George has a championship lock if it doesn't lose. Even with a loss or two, the team has a strong points lead over its nearest contenders, Beef Jerky in a Ball Gown and Psycho Slashers, giving Chris a 63% overall chance at making the playoffs. Good odds, those.

So You're Telling Me There's a Chance¶

It's been an unfortunate season for the Saint Louis Gorillas, who have scored the second-most points overall (after the Screw City Scramblers) and yet are 7th overall in the league standings. This contributes to the seemingly optimistic 29% of outcomes where the Gorillas make the playoffs. Hey, at least there's still waivers to look forward to.

Race to the Bottom¶

At this point, most of the bottom 4 teams still have at least a slim hope of climbing up to the playoffs. Good luck to the Psycho Slashers and Beef Jerky in a Ball Gown, both longshots for a playoff slot at 3% and 12% of projected outcomes, respectively.

Time for a Change¶

Snoz Berries had better do some retooling--they're currently most likely to finish last (67.32% of simluations). The Berries aren't getting their money back, either--this far back in the standings, they cannot hope to make it to the playoffs, and instead should focus on not getting last place.
