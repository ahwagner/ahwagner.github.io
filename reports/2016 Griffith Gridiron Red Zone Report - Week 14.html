def simulate_matchup(week, records, team1, team2):
    r1 = records[team1]
    r2 = records[team2]
    team1_mean = r1[2]/(week - 1)
    team2_mean = r2[2]/(week - 1)
    team1_score = round(np.random.normal(team1_mean, team1_mean / 10),2)
    team2_score = round(np.random.normal(team2_mean, team2_mean / 10),2)
    if team1_score > team2_score:
        a = 1
        b = 0
        winner = team1
        loser = team2
    elif team2_score > team1_score:
        a = 0
        b = 1
        winner = team2
        loser = team1
    else:
        return simulate_matchup(week, records, team1, team2)
    team1_result = (r1[0] + a, r1[1] + b, r1[2] + team1_score)
    team2_result = (r2[0] + b, r2[1] + a, r2[2] + team2_score)
    return { 'winner': winner,
             'loser': loser,
             'values': (team1_result, team2_result) }

def simulate_season_rank():
    new_records = records.copy()
    for week in range(COMPLETED_WEEKS + 1,15):
        for matchup in schedule[week]:
            r = simulate_matchup(week, new_records, *matchup)
            new_records[matchup[0]], new_records[matchup[1]] = r['values']
    r = new_records
    return sorted(r, key=lambda x: (r[x][0], r[x][2]), reverse=True)

def simulate_n(chunk_size):
    outcomes = {team: [] for team in records.keys()}
    playoff_wins = Counter()
    last_place_finish = Counter()
    
    for _ in range(chunk_size):
        r = simulate_ROS()
        playoff_wins[r[0]] += 1
        last_place_finish[r[1]] += 1
        for i, team in enumerate(r[2], start=1):
            outcomes[team].append(i)
    return playoff_wins, last_place_finish, outcomes

    last_place_finish += r[1]

Discussion¶

Winner's Circle¶

A Team Has No Name, My Name's Jeff, and the Deep Space Niners are now locked-in to the playoffs. Despite this, these teams can't rest easy, as positional ranking could play a large factor this year.

Lucky Ducks¶

Things could be particularly good for the #1 seed if Lee and the Assgard Mjlnirlicious can eke out a win this week, despite a first round pick (Cam Newton) looking worse than ever, and a second round pick (A.J. Green) potentially out for the remainder of the season. A weaker-than-average schedule has allowed them to make it this far, but things aren't looking too promising for the current #4 team.

Battle of the Spies¶

Team Always Blue! has proven themselves to be among the strongest teams in the league, and if Lee loses, is a very real threat to snatch the #4 seed. To do so, however, Nick needs to overcome the current #1 team, My Name's Jeff, headed up by fellow manager and younger brother Greg in their head-to-head matchup this week.

Well, that was fun¶

No Tengo Idea and House Lannister are effectively done for the season. These teams have no chance to make the playoffs, and no chance of being among the bottom 4. Solidly average work!

Panic Time¶

Password is Taco and Run CTE are now locked in for the Toilet Bowl. It's time for them to be thinking about weeks 15 and 16, as little can change for them this week.

Twin Titan Team Toilet Tussle¶

Blistering Barnacles and Thundering Typhoons are arguably in the most interesting head-to-head matchup, as the loser is guaranteed to go on to the toilet bowl. Blistering Barnacles is in double jeapordy, for even with a victory, a strong showing from Get Ziggy Wit it could ultimately drain their chances of avoiding the post-season plunge.
